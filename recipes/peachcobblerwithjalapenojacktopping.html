Peach Cobbler with Jalapeno Jack Topping

Recipe Peach Cobbler with Jalapeno Jack Topping

This is a delicious and unusual twist on peach cobbler. The spicy cheese adds a fantastic finish.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups peeled and sliced fresh peaches
  • 1 ¾ cups white sugar
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste
  • 1 cup shredded jalapeno Jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix peaches with 1 cup sugar in a bowl until coated; set aside. Combine flour, milk, 3/4 cup sugar, baking powder, and salt in another bowl.
  3. Pour butter into a 9x13-inch glass baking dish. Pour flour mixture over the melted butter. Spoon peaches over the flour, sprinkle generously with cinnamon, and sprinkle with shredded cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until filling is bubbly and cheese is golden brown, about 1 hour.
